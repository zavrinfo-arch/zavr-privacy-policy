About Zavr

Zavr is a goal-based savings tracking application designed to help users build consistent financial habits. Users can create goals, monitor progress, and collaborate through group savings.

Zavr is strictly a financial tracking tool and does not provide banking, lending, or investment services.

Information We Collect

  • User profile data (name, avatar)
  • Savings goals and activity data
  • Basic device and usage information

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank details or payment credentials.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To provide app functionality
  • To improve performance and experience
  • To maintain platform security

Data Storage & Retention

Your data is securely stored using trusted cloud infrastructure and retained only while your account is active.

Security

Third-Party Services
