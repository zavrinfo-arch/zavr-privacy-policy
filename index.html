5. Data Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data is encrypted when stored on our servers
  • Authentication Security: Passwords are hashed and salted (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

How to Request Deletion:
